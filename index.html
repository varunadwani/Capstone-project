Welcome to the Grand Theft Auto Series

Grand Theft Auto (GTA) is one of the most influential and controversial video game franchises ever created. Developed by Rockstar Games, the series places players in sprawling open-world cities teeming with life, opportunity, and danger. Each title invites exploration, from the sun-soaked streets of Vice City to the urban sprawl of Los Santos.

Known for its cinematic storytelling, memorable characters, and freedom of choice, GTA lets players experience a vast criminal universe. Missions blend action, humor, drama, and high-octane chases, while a living world of side activities, collectibles, and secrets brings each city to life.

The GTA series has consistently pushed the boundaries of technology and creativity, breaking sales records and shaping the pop culture landscape. Whether you’re soaking in the neon lights of a city at night or embarking on a wild heist, GTA always offers an unforgettable adventure.
